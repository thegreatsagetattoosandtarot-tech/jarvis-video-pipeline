# Revenue Tracking

## KPI Formulas
### Average Ticket
`AVG_TICKET = TOTAL_REVENUE / COMPLETED_BOOKINGS`

### Monthly Recurring Revenue
`MRR = SUM(TAROT_MONTHLY_SUBSCRIPTIONS) * SUBSCRIPTION_PRICE`

### Client Lifetime Value
`LTV = SUM(CLIENT_REVENUE) / COUNT(UNIQUE_CLIENTS)`

### Revenue per Artist
`REV_PER_ARTIST = ARTIST_REVENUE / ARTIST_BOOKINGS`

### Utilization Rate
`UTILIZATION = BOOKED_HOURS / AVAILABLE_HOURS`

### Client Acquisition Cost
`CAC = MARKETING_SPEND / NEW_CLIENTS`

### Retention Rate (6-month)
`RETENTION = CLIENTS_RETURNING_6MO / TOTAL_CLIENTS * 100`

## Tax Estimation
### Quarterly Estimated Payments
`QUARTERLY_TAX = NET_INCOME_QUARTER * 0.25` (conservative 25%)

### Annual Tax Reserve
`ANNUAL_RESERVE = YTD_NET_INCOME * 0.30` (30% for safety)
